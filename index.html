About

My name is Carlos Mayo. I am a self-taught web developer and computer science student originally from Venezuela and currently based in Miami, FL. I see software development the same way a blacksmith sees his craft, and someday I hope to create the greatest of swords.

I'm currently working through the curriculum over at FreeCodeCamp, polishing my front-end web skills and learning back-end development with Node.js.

I love problem solving and reading and learning about a bunch of stuff, but especially science and programming. My goals are to advance my career, build great products, and eventually master my craft. I want my skills and knowledge in technology to solve real world challenges. When I am not immersed in all things web, I can be found reading, making outdoor activities or just eating an arepa :) - follow my journey as I create my path for awesomeness!
